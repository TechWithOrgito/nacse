New Blog #1 — Presenting NACSE

NACSE — the Newsletter of Applied Cybersecurity & Systems Engineering — was created to explore cybersecurity and systems engineering as they are practiced in real environments.

The newsletter focuses on real systems, engineering decisions, and the security outcomes that emerge from architecture and operational context.

NACSE serves as an accessible entry point for applied discussions that may later evolve into deeper work within JACSE.
